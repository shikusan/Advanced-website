History

The Maasai Mara was established in 1961 as a wildlife sanctuary before being declared a national reserve.

Best Times to Visit

  1. July – October: Great Migration
  2. December – February: Predator sightings
  3. March – June: Lush landscapes after rains

Key Terms

Big Five
Lion, Leopard, Elephant, Rhino, Buffalo
Great Migration
Annual movement of wildebeest and zebras across the Mara River
